Madhubani Art (Mithila Painting)

Hailing from Bihar, Madhubani features striking, two-dimensional geometric arrangements drawn without prior tracing lines. Created by rural women using twigs, matchsticks, and clean finger techniques, it features line fillers (Kachni) and solid value fills (Bharni) derived from natural plant sources.
