Phad Paintings (Rajasthani Epic Scroll Art)

Phad is a continuous scroll painting system from Rajasthan used by traveling Bhopa bards to narrate local epics. Hand-painted on thick starched canvas boards with vibrant hues, these compositions feature side-facing character orientations where figures face each other rather than the viewer.
