Pithora Art (Rathwa Tribal Ritual Wall Sheets)

Practiced by the Rathwa tribe of Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh, Pithora is a sacred ritual art form rather than a purely decorative style. Traditionally painted on agricultural walls to thank Baba Pithora for prosperity, these compositions feature a distinct rectangular border enclosure filled with stylized multi-colored horses.
