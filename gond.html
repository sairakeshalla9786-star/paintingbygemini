Gond Art (Madhya Pradesh Tribal Folklore)

Created by the Gond tribe of Central India, this art form uses complex textures made of repetitive dots, dashes, and herringbone lines. These patterns form vibrant compositions of interconnected birds, sacred deer, and stylized trees, transforming local flora and fauna into a psychedelic tapestry of folklore.
