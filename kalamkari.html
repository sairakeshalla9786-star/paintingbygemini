Kalamkari Art (Srikalahasti & Machilipatnam Styles)

Derived from the Persian words 'Kalam' (pen) and 'Kari' (craftsmanship), Kalamkari is a 3,000-year-old organic textile art using sharp bamboo pens, natural vegetable-extracted mordants, and continuous river washings to depict classical epics and dense biological trees of life.
