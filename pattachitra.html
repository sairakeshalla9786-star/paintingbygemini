Pattachitra Paintings (Odisha Heritage Canvas Scrolls)

Hailing from the heritage village of Raghurajpur, Odisha, Pattachitra is a structured, cloth-based scroll painting system closely tied to the cult of Lord Jagannath. Built on canvas sheets prepared with chalk and tamarind seed paste, it features distinct profile eyes, decorative borders, and vibrant natural mineral colors.
